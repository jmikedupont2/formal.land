Make Tezos the first formally verified cryptocurrency

February 2, 2022 Â· 4 min read

Our primary goal at FormalÂ LandÂ ğŸŒ² is to make Tezos the first crypto-currency with a formally verified implementation. With formal verification, thanks to mathematical methods, we can check that a program behaves as expected for all possible inputs. Formal verification goes beyond what testing can do, as testing can only handle a finite amount of cases.
